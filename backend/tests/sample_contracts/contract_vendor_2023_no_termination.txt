VENDOR AGREEMENT

This Vendor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of July 1, 2023,
by and between:

CloudVendor Solutions AG, a company incorporated under the laws of Switzerland,
with its registered office at Bahnhofstrasse 12, 8001 Zürich, Switzerland
("Vendor"),

and

Riverty GmbH, a company incorporated under the laws of Germany, with its registered
office at Olof-Palme-Straße 4, 40882 Ratingen, Germany ("Customer").


1. SUPPLY OF PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

1.1 The Vendor agrees to supply the Customer with cloud infrastructure services,
software licences, and associated technical support ("Products and Services") as
further described in the Product Schedule attached hereto as Exhibit A.

1.2 The Vendor shall ensure that all Products and Services conform to the
specifications set out in Exhibit A and meet the service level standards described
in Exhibit B ("Service Level Agreement" or "SLA").

1.3 The Vendor reserves the right to update or modify the Products and Services,
provided that such modifications do not materially diminish the functionality or
performance levels agreed in the SLA.


2. PRICING AND INVOICING

2.1 The Customer shall pay the Vendor in accordance with the pricing schedule set
out in Exhibit A. All prices are stated in euros and are exclusive of VAT.

2.2 The Vendor shall issue invoices on a quarterly basis. Payment is due within
thirty (30) days of the invoice date.

2.3 The Vendor may adjust pricing annually with sixty (60) days prior written
notice to the Customer, provided that any increase does not exceed five percent
(5%) per annum.


3. DATA PROTECTION AND GDPR COMPLIANCE

3.1 The Parties acknowledge that the performance of this Agreement may involve the
processing of personal data as defined under Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (the "General
Data Protection Regulation" or "GDPR").

3.2 To the extent that the Vendor processes personal data on behalf of the Customer
in connection with the Products and Services, the Vendor shall act as a data
processor within the meaning of Article 28 GDPR, and the Customer shall act as
the data controller.

3.3 The Vendor warrants that it shall: (a) process personal data only on documented
instructions from the Customer; (b) ensure that all personnel authorised to process
personal data are bound by appropriate confidentiality obligations; (c) implement
and maintain appropriate technical and organisational security measures in accordance
with Article 32 GDPR; and (d) not engage any sub-processor without the prior written
consent of the Customer.

3.4 The Vendor agrees to maintain its data processing facilities within the European
Economic Area (EEA) or in countries that have been granted an adequacy decision by
the European Commission, ensuring that all personal data transfers comply with Chapter
V of the GDPR.

3.5 The Vendor shall notify the Customer without undue delay, and in any event within
48 hours, of becoming aware of any personal data breach affecting the Customer's data.
Such notification shall include sufficient information to enable the Customer to fulfil
its own notification obligations under Article 33 GDPR.


4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

4.1 The Vendor retains all intellectual property rights in the Products and Services,
including any software, tools, or methodologies developed or used in connection with
the delivery of the Products and Services.

4.2 Subject to the Customer's payment of all applicable fees, the Vendor grants the
Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable licence to use the Products and
Services solely for the Customer's internal business purposes during the term of this
Agreement.


5. LIABILITY CAP

5.1 In no event shall either Party be liable to the other for any indirect, incidental,
special, punitive, or consequential damages arising out of or in connection with this
Agreement, including but not limited to loss of revenue, loss of profits, loss of
business, or loss of data.

5.2 Notwithstanding any other provision of this Agreement, the total aggregate liability
of either Party to the other under or in connection with this Agreement, whether arising
in contract, tort (including negligence), breach of statutory duty, or otherwise, shall
in no event exceed EUR 100,000 (one hundred thousand euros) in any twelve-month period.

5.3 Nothing in this Agreement shall limit or exclude either Party's liability for: (a)
death or personal injury caused by negligence; (b) fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation;
or (c) any liability that cannot be excluded or limited by applicable law.


6. WARRANTIES

6.1 The Vendor warrants that the Products and Services will perform substantially in
accordance with the specifications in Exhibit A for the duration of this Agreement.

6.2 If a defect is identified in the Products or Services, the Vendor's sole obligation
shall be to use reasonable efforts to correct the defect within the timeframes set out
in the SLA.

6.3 Except as expressly stated in this Agreement, all warranties, conditions, and terms
implied by statute or common law are excluded to the fullest extent permitted by law.


7. GOVERNING LAW

7.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of
Germany, excluding the United Nations Convention on Contracts for the International
Sale of Goods.

7.2 Any disputes arising from or related to this Agreement shall be subject to the
exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Düsseldorf, Germany.

7.3 The Parties agree to attempt to resolve any dispute through good-faith negotiations
for a period of thirty (30) days before commencing legal proceedings.
